Our Process

Every sourcing engagement follows a structured six-step process.

Step 01

Requirement Analysis

We define compound specifications, purity targets, quantity, timeline, and any regulatory parameters with your team.

Step 02

Manufacturer Identification

We identify qualified GMP-certified manufacturers from our network with demonstrated capability for your compound class.

Step 03

Supplier Qualification

We verify certifications, audit documentation, review inspection history, and confirm batch traceability.

Step 04

Order Management

We manage procurement, documentation, and logistics from manufacturer to your receiving facility.

Step 05

Analytical Verification

Every delivery undergoes independent third-party analysis — HPLC, MS, or NMR — before release to your team.

Step 06

Ongoing Partnership

We maintain relationships with multiple qualified manufacturers per compound class for supply chain resilience.
