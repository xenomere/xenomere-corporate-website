Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

Xenomere OÜ (registry code 17507482) is a private limited company registered in the Republic of Estonia at Narva mnt 5, 10117 Tallinn, Estonia. For privacy-related enquiries, contact us at support@xenomere.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Information you provide: name, organisation, job title, email address, telephone number, and details of your compound sourcing requirements when you submit an inquiry or engage with our services.
  • Information collected automatically: IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited when you access our website.
  • Cookie data: as described in our Cookie Policy.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Respond to inquiries and sourcing requests
  • Communicate about orders, logistics, and service delivery
  • Manage ongoing business relationships
  • Improve our services and website
  • Comply with applicable Estonian and European Union law

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

4. Legal Basis

We process personal data under the following legal bases as defined by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Contractual necessity: to perform sourcing agreements and respond to inquiries
  • Legitimate interest: to operate and improve our business, maintain security, and communicate with partners
  • Legal obligation: to comply with tax, accounting, and regulatory requirements
  • Consent: where required for optional analytics cookies or marketing communications

5. Data Sharing

We may share personal data with the following categories of recipients, all located within the EU or EEA:

  • Logistics and shipping partners for order fulfilment
  • Analytical laboratories for compound verification
  • IT infrastructure and hosting providers

We do not sell, trade, or share personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

  • Inquiry data: retained for 3 years from the date of last contact
  • Transactional records: retained for 7 years in accordance with Estonian accounting and tax law
  • Analytics data: retained for 26 months

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data, including SSL/TLS encryption, access controls, regular security assessments, and EU-based server infrastructure.

8. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, email support@xenomere.com. The supervisory authority for Estonia is the Estonian Data Protection Inspectorate (www.aki.ee).

9. International Transfers

Personal data is stored on servers located within the European Union. Where data must be transferred outside the EU/EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version is always available at xenomere.com/privacy.
