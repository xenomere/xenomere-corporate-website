Returns & Refunds

1. Return Policy

Returns are handled on a per-engagement basis as defined in individual sourcing agreements. All return eligibility is assessed against the agreed quality specifications documented in the Certificate of Analysis.

2. Quality Guarantee

Every compound undergoes independent third-party analytical verification — HPLC, mass spectrometry, or NMR — before release. We guarantee that delivered compounds meet the specifications agreed in the Certificate of Analysis for that engagement.

3. Eligible Returns

Returns may be accepted in the following circumstances:

  • Compound does not match agreed analytical specifications
  • Material damaged during transit
  • Wrong compound delivered
  • Temperature excursion during shipping where compound stability was compromised

4. Non-Eligible Returns

Returns will not be accepted for:

  • Change of mind after receipt
  • Improper storage after receipt by the buyer
  • Compound meets specifications but is unsuitable for the buyer's specific application
  • Customs or import issues arising from the buyer's failure to obtain required permits

5. Return Process

To initiate a return, email support@xenomere.com within 48 hours of receipt with:

  • Order reference number
  • Compound name
  • Reason for return
  • Photographs of packaging and compound where applicable

Do not return any material without written authorisation from Xenomere OÜ.

6. Refunds

Approved refunds are processed via SEPA bank transfer within 14 business days of verification. Full refunds are issued for specification failures or transit damage confirmed by our quality review process.

7. Disputes

Quality disputes are resolved through binding third-party laboratory testing. The party whose claim is not substantiated by independent analysis bears the cost of testing.

8. Contact

For returns and refund enquiries, contact support@xenomere.com.
