About Xenomere

Bridging advanced R&D and the world's leading chemical manufacturers.

Our Story

Xenomere OÜ is an Estonian-registered chemical and biochemical sourcing company founded in 2026. We bridge the gap between advanced R&D organisations and leading chemical and pharmaceutical manufacturers across Europe.

Our team identifies, vets, and partners with GMP-certified manufacturers to source high-purity compounds for research, development, and testing applications. By managing supplier qualification, documentation, and logistics, we enable our clients to focus on their core research.

Mission

To provide research organisations with reliable, documented, and analytically verified access to high-purity chemical and biochemical compounds through transparent, compliant supply chains.

Our Values

Transparency

Every supplier qualification, every certificate, every analytical report is shared with our partners. Complete visibility into the sourcing process.

Quality Without Compromise

If a compound does not meet the agreed analytical specification, it does not ship. Our independent verification policy is non-negotiable.

Regulatory Integrity

We operate strictly within EU legal frameworks. Every transaction is documented, traceable, and compliant with applicable regulations.
