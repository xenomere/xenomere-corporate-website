Research Use Disclaimer

1. Intended Use

All chemical and biochemical compounds sourced through Xenomere OÜ are intended exclusively for:

  • In vitro laboratory research
  • Analytical method development
  • Reference standards for quality control
  • QC testing and validation
  • Research and development by qualified professionals

2. NOT for Human or Veterinary Use

Compounds sourced through Xenomere OÜ are not intended for:

  • Human consumption or administration
  • Therapeutic or diagnostic use in humans
  • Veterinary use or animal treatment
  • Food, dietary supplements, or nutraceutical applications
  • Cosmetic applications (unless explicitly agreed in writing)

3. Buyer Responsibility

By engaging with Xenomere OÜ, the buyer confirms that they are a qualified professional or authorised institution and agree to:

  • Handle all compounds in accordance with the provided Safety Data Sheet
  • Determine the suitability of compounds for their intended research application
  • Comply with all applicable local, national, and international laws and regulations
  • Not resell compounds to consumers or unqualified parties
  • Assume full liability for the use, storage, and disposal of sourced compounds

4. Regulatory Compliance

Buyers located outside the European Union are solely responsible for ensuring the lawfulness of import, obtaining required permits and licences, and complying with local regulations governing controlled substances, hazardous materials, and research chemicals.

5. No Medical Claims

Nothing on this website constitutes medical advice, a therapeutic recommendation, or an endorsement of any compound for clinical use. Xenomere OÜ makes no claims regarding the efficacy, safety, or suitability of any compound for human or veterinary applications.

6. Contact

For questions regarding intended use or regulatory requirements, contact support@xenomere.com.
