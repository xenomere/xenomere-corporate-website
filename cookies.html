Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function properly and to provide information to website operators.

2. How We Use Cookies

Xenomere OÜ uses cookies for essential website functionality only. Our approach is deliberately minimal.

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function and cannot be disabled:

  • Session cookie: maintains your session while browsing the website
  • Cookie preference: stores your cookie consent choice (retained for 12 months)
  • CSRF token: protects form submissions against cross-site request forgery

Optional Analytics Cookies

With your consent, we may use anonymous analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our website. These cookies:

  • Do not identify you personally
  • Are only activated with your explicit consent
  • Are not shared with advertising networks

Cookies We Do Not Use

We do not use cookies for:

  • Advertising or retargeting
  • Social media tracking
  • Browser fingerprinting

3. Third-Party Cookies

We do not use third-party cookies. All website resources are self-hosted. The only external resource loaded is the Inter typeface from Google Fonts, which may set cookies subject to Google's own policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookie preferences through our consent banner when first visiting the website. You may also configure your browser to refuse all or certain cookies. Note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may affect website functionality.

5. Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The current version is always available at xenomere.com/cookies.
