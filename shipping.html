Shipping & Delivery

1. Shipping Policy

All shipments are arranged from the manufacturing facility to the partner's designated receiving location. Xenomere OÜ manages logistics coordination, carrier selection, and documentation for every delivery as part of our sourcing service.

2. Shipping Origins

Compounds are shipped from GMP-certified manufacturing facilities located in Germany, Spain, Belgium, the United Kingdom, and other EU/EEA countries within our supplier network.

3. Destinations

We deliver to EU member states, EEA countries, Switzerland, and the United Kingdom. Deliveries to other destinations are evaluated on a case-by-case basis, subject to export control requirements and applicable licensing.

4. Shipping Methods

Shipping methods are selected based on compound stability requirements, regulatory classification, destination, and urgency. Where required, we utilise temperature-controlled transport and dangerous goods certified carriers to ensure compound integrity during transit.

5. Costs

Shipping costs are quoted per individual sourcing agreement. Pricing is based on shipment weight, temperature control requirements, regulatory classification, destination, and insurance coverage.

6. Timeline

Delivery timelines depend on manufacturing lead time, analytical verification, transit duration, and customs processing where applicable. Expedited shipping options are available for time-sensitive requirements and will be discussed during the sourcing engagement.

7. Receiving

Upon receipt of a delivery, partners should:

  • Inspect packaging for damage or tampering
  • Verify contents against the packing list
  • Check temperature indicators where applicable
  • Store compounds according to SDS and COA specifications
  • Report any issues within 48 hours to support@xenomere.com

8. Documentation

Every shipment includes the following documentation:

  • Certificate of Analysis (COA)
  • Safety Data Sheet (SDS)
  • Packing list
  • Chain of custody records
  • Customs documentation (for non-EU destinations)
  • Import permits where required
